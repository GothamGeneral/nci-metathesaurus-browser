NCI Metathesaurus (NCIm) is a wide-ranging biomedical terminology database that covers most vocabulary used by NCI for clinical care, translational and basic research, and public information and administrative activities.

NCIm features:
  • Maps 4,600,000 terms from 70 sources into 1,300,000 biomedical concepts that represent their meaning;
  • Preferred terms, synonyms, definitions, and other information from each source;
  • Links to NCI Thesaurus and other information sources;
  • Over 200,000 cross-links between concepts, providing formal logic-based definition of many concepts;
  • 17,000,000 cross-links between content elements; and
  • Updated frequently by a team of biomedical terminology and subject matter experts.
NCIm contains most public domain vocabularies from the National Library of Medicine's UMLS Metathesaurus, as well as many other biomedical vocabularies created by or of interest to NCI and its partners. Some propriety vocabularies are included, with permission, and have restrictions on their use.
